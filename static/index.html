The Problem

Most productivity tools ask:
"What do you need to do?"

Reflecta asks something more dangerous:

"What state of mind created this thought?"

We're tired of to-do list anxiety — the endless loop of adding tasks without understanding why we feel overwhelmed by them.

The Science

Metacognition:
Your Brain's Superpower

The human brain can monitor and regulate its own thought processes — but it's the first thing that breaks under stress.

Pattern Recognition

When Reflecta identifies "Anxious" entries over 14 days, it isn't just showing data — it's acting as an externalized prefrontal cortex.

Emotional Regulation

By externalizing and labeling an emotion, you reduce its power over you. Recognition is the first step to control.

Cognitive Load

The average person holds 7±2 items in working memory. Writing externalizes the load, freeing your mind to think clearly.

Self-Awareness at Scale

Reflecta helps you recognize the root cause of burnout before it happens. Prevention through pattern visibility.
